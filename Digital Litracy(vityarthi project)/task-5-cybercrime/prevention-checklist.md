### Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students



#### General Safety Tips:

1. ##### Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps.
2. ##### Never share OTPs or passwords with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank.
3. ##### Verify links before clicking – Check the URL carefully for fake websites.
4. ##### Download apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or App Store.
5. ##### Keep your devices updated – Install software and security updates regularly.
6. ##### Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive work like banking or logging into important accounts.
7. ##### Log out from shared/public computers after use.
8. ##### Limit personal information shared online (address, phone number, college ID, etc.).

#### 

#### Financial \& UPI Safety Tips:

1. ##### Never approve unknown UPI collect requests (Request/Pay alerts on apps like GPay/PhonePe).
2. ##### Do not scan QR codes to receive money – Scanning is only for paying, not receiving.
3. ##### Set a UPI PIN that is hard to guess and never share it with anyone.

#### 

#### If Something Goes Wrong:

1. ##### Immediately report cyber fraud at the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
2. ##### Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (for quick action in financial fraud cases)
3. ##### Inform your bank/app immediately to block transactions if fraud occurs.
