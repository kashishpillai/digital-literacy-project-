### 5 Do's and 5 Don'ts of Social Media as a College Student.



#### **5 Do's:**

* ##### Be respectful – Communicate politely and avoid offensive language.
* ##### Use social media for learning – Follow educational pages and useful content.
* ##### Stay aware of cyber security – Avoid clicking suspicious links.
* ##### Think before you post – Make sure your content is appropriate and respectful.
* ##### Protect your privacy – Use strong passwords and privacy settings.



#### **5 Don'ts:**

* ##### **Don’t engage in cyberbullying – Avoid harassing or insulting others online.**
* ##### **Don’t overshare – Keep private matters offline.**
* ##### **Don’t post inappropriate content – It can harm your reputation.**
* ##### **Don’t accept unknown friend requests – Be cautious of strangers.**
* ##### **Don’t rely completely on social media – Balance real-life activities too.**
